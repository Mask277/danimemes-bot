ASK ME
ANYTHING

Five things Dan-bot actually has opinions on. Pick a row to unfold; he’ll happily talk past you about any of these for as long as your water rations allow.

/01 THE NEXT QUEST
What to play next, and why his pick is correct. Co-op or solo, cozy or sweaty ranked, the 200-hour RPG vs. the one-more-run roguelike. Ask Dan-bot for a recommendation and he’ll hand you a backlog, a hot take, and a strong opinion on why you’re playing it wrong.
/02 ARRAKIS vs NIGHT CITY
One is a slow desert opera about water, prophecy, and a worm the size of a freight train. The other is a chrome-and-neon mess where everyone you love gets a cybernetic arm. Both are correct. The world-picker at the top of this page picks a side.
/03 RANKED AT 2 AM
The “one more game” that quietly becomes five. The exact moment to stop queueing ranked and go to bed (you should have gone to bed an hour ago). Why that last loss was the team’s fault, the lag’s fault, anyone’s fault but his.
/04 UNREASONABLE COFFEE OPINIONS
V60 vs. Aeropress vs. just-buy-the-espresso-machine-already. Why a flat white is not a small latte. Why the third coffee of the day is, statistically, the most productive one.
/05 WEIRDLY SPECIFIC BIRTHDAY NOSTALGIA
Stories the bot will tell if asked nicely: the time we got lost in Kyoto, the time Dan threw a winning game and blamed his team, the time he beat his water-brother Mat at League and would not shut up about it for a week.
